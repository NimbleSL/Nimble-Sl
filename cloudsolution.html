Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our cloud solutions and services.

How long does a cloud migration typically take?

Cloud migration timelines vary based on the complexity of your infrastructure and applications. Simple migrations can take 1-2 months, while large enterprise migrations may take 6-12 months or more. We begin with a thorough assessment to provide you with a realistic timeline based on your specific environment.

Which cloud provider is best for my business?

The best cloud provider depends on your specific requirements, existing technology stack, budget, and goals. AWS offers the most comprehensive service portfolio, Azure provides strong integration with Microsoft products, and Google Cloud excels in data analytics and machine learning. We help you evaluate your needs and recommend the most suitable provider or even a multi-cloud approach if appropriate.

How do you ensure security in the cloud?

We implement a comprehensive security strategy that includes identity and access management, network security, encryption, security monitoring, and compliance controls. We follow cloud security best practices and industry standards such as the AWS Well-Architected Framework and CIS Benchmarks. Our team regularly performs security assessments and stays updated on the latest security threats and solutions.

How do you help optimize cloud costs?

We employ several strategies for cloud cost optimization, including right-sizing resources, leveraging reserved instances or savings plans, implementing auto-scaling, identifying and removing unused resources, and setting up detailed cost monitoring and alerting. We provide regular cost optimization recommendations and work with you to balance performance needs with budget constraints.

What level of support do you provide after migration?

We offer different tiers of managed services to support your cloud environment after migration. Our standard support includes 24/7 monitoring, incident response, regular maintenance, and monthly reporting. For more comprehensive support, our premium managed services include proactive optimization, advanced security monitoring, and dedicated support engineers. We tailor our support offerings to meet your specific needs and budget.
