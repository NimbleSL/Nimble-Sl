## Best Practices

1. **Personalize first line** - mention something specific about their company
2. **Send 20-30 connection requests daily**
3. **Best times to send**: Tue-Thu, 9-11am their timezone
4. **Follow up once** after 5-7 days, then stop
5. **Don't pitch immediately** - connect first, pitch in follow-up message
6. **Track in spreadsheet**: Name, Company, Date Sent, Response, Status
