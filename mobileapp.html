Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our mobile app development services.

How long does it take to develop a mobile app?

Timeline varies based on complexity - simple apps may take 2-3 months, while complex applications can take 4-6 months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a more precise estimate based on your specific requirements.

Should I build a native or cross-platform app?

The choice depends on your specific needs. Native apps offer the best performance and access to platform features but require separate development for iOS and Android. Cross-platform solutions like React Native or Flutter provide cost efficiency with a single codebase while still maintaining good performance. We can help you determine the best approach based on your goals, budget, and timeline.

How much does it cost to develop a mobile app?

Mobile app costs vary significantly based on complexity, features, and platforms supported. Simple apps might start around $25,000-$50,000, while complex applications with advanced features can range from $75,000-$250,000+. We provide transparent pricing after understanding your exact requirements.

How do you handle app store submissions?

We manage the entire app store submission process for both Apple App Store and Google Play Store. This includes preparing all required assets, optimizing store listings, ensuring compliance with platform guidelines, and addressing any feedback from the review process. Our goal is to make the launch process as smooth as possible.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and updates?

Yes, we offer various maintenance packages to ensure your app remains secure, compatible with new OS versions, and performing optimally. These services include bug fixes, performance optimization, OS compatibility updates, and implementation of new features based on user feedback and evolving business needs.
