What is Staff Augmentation?

Staff augmentation means hiring dedicated remote developers who work as an extension of your existing team. Unlike outsourcing entire projects, you maintain full control over management, processes, and direction. We provide skilled developers from around the world.

  • Your Team, Extended: Developers work exclusively for you, attend your standups, use your tools
  • Full Control: You manage tasks, priorities, and daily work directly
  • Flexible Scaling: Add or reduce team members based on project needs
  • Cost Effective: 40-60% lower than hiring locally, no recruitment costs
